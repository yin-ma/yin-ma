Collision Dectection

The steps to detect collisions between two objects are as follows:

1. Define a ground.

2. Project the shadows of the objects onto the ground.

3. Check for any overlap between the two shadows.

Now, we will go through different types of collisions one by one to demonstrate how the algorithm works.
